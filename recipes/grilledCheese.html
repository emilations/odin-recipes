Grilled Cheese

Description:

A grilled cheese sandwhich is made from melting cheddar cheese onto toast bread. This sandwich is very popular in North America.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 slices of cheese

Steps:

  1. Butter both sides of the toast generously.
  2. Cook the toast on medium heat using a skillet.
  3. Once toasted, add the grilled cheese on one side and wait till it melts.
