Poutine

Description:

Poutine is a French Canadian tradition comfort food.

Ingredients:

  • Potato
  • Gravy sause
  • Cubed white cheddar cheese

Steps:

  1. Cut the potato to make french fries.
  2. Put the cooked french fries into a bowl and cover the top with cheese.
  3. Pour heated 80C gravy sause on top of the fries and cheese.
